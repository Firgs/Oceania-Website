How can you help

Volunteering with Oceania offers a range of opportunities to make a tangible difference in protecting our oceans. Here are several ways you can get involved:

Beach and River Cleanups

Join our organized cleanup events to remove plastic and other waste from coastal and riverine areas. These cleanups play a crucial role in preventing waste from entering our oceans.

Community Education and Outreach

Help spread awareness by participating in or leading educational workshops, school programs, and community talks. Share information about the impact of plastic pollution and ways to reduce it.

Advocacy and Campaigns

Support our advocacy efforts by participating in campaigns, signing petitions, or engaging with policymakers to promote ocean-friendly policies and regulations.
