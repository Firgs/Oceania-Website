About us

Welcome to Oceania, a dedicated Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) passionately committed to raising awareness about the escalating crisis of plastic pollution in our oceans. Our mission is to illuminate the intricate connections between plastic and microplastic contamination, the preservation of marine species, and the overarching impact on human health.

At Oceania, we believe that the health of our oceans is inextricably linked to the well-being of all life on Earth. Our oceans are not just vast expanses of water; they are a crucial part of our global ecosystem, playing a key role in climate regulation, supporting diverse marine life, and providing vital resources for human survival.

However, the relentless influx of plastics and microplastics into these precious waters poses a dire threat. These pollutants not only harm marine life, often leading to the decline or extinction of species, but they also cycle back to us, affecting human health through the food chain and water sources. Our team at Oceania is dedicated to shedding light on these issues through rigorous research, educational outreach, and active engagement with communities and policymakers.

We are driven by the vision of a world where our oceans are free from the shackles of pollution, where marine life thrives in harmony, and where future generations can enjoy the bounties of a clean and healthy ocean.

Our values

  • Integrity

    Integrity is the bedrock of Oceania. We uphold honesty and transparency in all our actions and communications. This commitment to ethical conduct ensures that we remain a trusted and credible voice in marine conservation, fostering a culture of respect and accountability.

  • Commitment

    Our unwavering commitment drives us to protect and preserve the oceans. We are dedicated to relentless advocacy, innovative research, and impactful action. This deep-rooted commitment underlines our efforts to engage communities and stakeholders in our mission for a healthier marine ecosystem.

  • Solidarity

    Solidarity fuels our collaborative efforts at Oceania. We believe in the power of unity and cooperation in tackling oceanic challenges. Embracing diverse partnerships and fostering a sense of community, we stand together in our quest to safeguard the oceans for future generations.

Our goals

At Oceania, our goals are driven by a deep commitment to preserving the health and integrity of our planet's oceans. We are dedicated to addressing the pressing challenges posed by plastic pollution and its impact on marine ecosystems, wildlife, and human health. Our key goals include:

  • Reducing Plastic Pollution:Our primary aim is to significantly decrease the amount of plastic entering the oceans.

  • Educational Outreach: Educating the public, especially the younger generation, about the dangers of plastic pollution is paramount.

  • Protecting Marine Biodiversity: Our efforts extend to safeguarding the diverse species that call the oceans home.

  • Influencing Policy and Legislation: We actively engage with policymakers and governmental bodies to advocate for laws and regulations that support ocean conservation, reduce plastic production, and improve waste management systems.

  • Promoting Healthy Oceans for Future Generations: Our overarching goal is to ensure that future generations inherit oceans that are vibrant, clean, and teeming with life.
